About Us

Think Global. Act Local.

NayePankh Foundation was born during the COVID-19 crisis with a mission to support communities in need. Today, it works across education, hunger relief, hygiene awareness, clothing distribution, animal welfare, and youth empowerment.

Mission

Empower communities through awareness, education and social development.

Vision

A society where every individual has dignity, opportunity and hope.
